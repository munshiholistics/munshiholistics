🌿 Understanding Gut Health

Our digestive system is a team player non-stop ensuring all systems work. 70% of our immune system is housed in our gut!

Benefits of a Healthy Gut

  • Healthy Skin: Lymph nodes are activated; natural glow achieved.
  • Strong Hair: Active sweat glands excrete toxins, nourishing the scalp.
  • Disease Prevention: Helps prevent Cancers, Diabetes 2, Constipation, Acidity, Depression/Insomnia, and aids Autism/Down Syndrome by stabilizing moods.

Pointers to a Bad Gut

  • Upset Stomach & Food Intolerance
  • High Sugar Diet & Slow Metabolism
  • Unintentional Weight Changes
  • Sleep Disturbance & Constant Fatigue
  • Skin Irritation & Autoimmune Conditions

🩸 Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes affects how your body uses sugar (glucose), essential for fueling muscles and the brain. Managing glucose is key to preventing complications.

Potential Complications

  • Cardiovascular Diseases: Increased risk of heart attack, stroke, heart failure, and atherosclerosis.
  • Nerve Damage (Neuropathy): High blood sugar can damage tiny vessels, leading to tingling or numbness.
  • Kidney Damage: High blood sugar in your kidneys can damage their tiny vessels. They also have an increased kidney damage and kidney failure.
  • Foot Damage: Poor circulation and neuropathy increase risk of infections and poor healing.
